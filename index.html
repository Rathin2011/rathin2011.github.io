About Me

I am a final-year PhD student working under the supervision of Mark Bun at Boston University. Previously, I was a research intern at the National University of Singapore working with Prof. Arnab Bhattacharyya and Prof. Ioannis Panageas.

Before that, I interned at Microsoft Research India with Dr. Amit Sharma. I completed my undergraduate studies at the University of Pune, majoring in Computer Engineering. During my undergrad, I was fortunate to work with Dr. Piyush Srivastava at TIFR and Dr. Yogesh Wadadekar and Dr. C.H. Ishwara Chandra at NCRA-TIFR.

My research interests broadly lie in theoretical computer science, including statistical and computational learning theory, distribution testing, and privacy.
